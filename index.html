About Me

Programming is my long-undiscovered passion.

I was renovating houses in Seattle when a friend sent me a link to a online coding course, along with a simple message: "I think you'd be good at this." Four months, one Harvard summer class and one General Assembly immersive program later, it's safe to say that my friend was right. I have taken to writing code like a fish to water.

In addition to my recent intensive training as a full-stack web developer, I have worked as a researcher, a youth mentor, and a project manager. My ability to adapt to new challenges makes me a valuable contributor to any team in the fast-paced world of development.
